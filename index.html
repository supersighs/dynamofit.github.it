Be a Dynamo

What is Dynamo Fitness?

Dynamo Fitness is a private fitness gym in Pflugerville, Texas. It is operated by Keri Klumker, certified Personal Trainer, Corrective Exercise Specialist and Fitness Nutrition Specialist. All workouts are specifically made for EACH client and held in a private gym and comfortable environment. Workouts are tailored to meet individual goals and preferences. Safety, proper form and achievable goals are the core of my training programs.

Meet your trainer

My passion has always been helping people. I graduated nursing school with the thought of helping people get well. 10 years later I decided to go another route and help people stay out of the hospital altogether. I had always loved to workout, loved the feeling of bettering myself. I also fell in love with weight lifting. As I pursued my love for lifting, it became very clear that most women didn’t know the benefits of lifting, were intimidated by it and the gym setting as well. It became my mission to teach both men, women and kids how to properly and safely lift weights. I now have a fully loaded garage gym, I am a certified Personal Trainer and I LOVE the time spent in my gym with my clients of all levels and ages.

Our Facility

Here at Dynamo Fitness you will find a fully loaded private gym capable of of handling your every need. From dumbbells to benches, racks to rowers, we can create whatever workout that is right for you. Mirrors surround the gym to help improve your form and showcase your hard work, music is always playing and your personalized and custom workouts will always be up on the board when you arrive.
