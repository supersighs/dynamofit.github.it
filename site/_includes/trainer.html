Meet your trainer

My passion has always been helping people. I graduated nursing school with the thought of helping people get well. 10 years later I decided to go another route and help people stay out of the hospital altogether. I had always loved to workout, loved the feeling of bettering myself. I also fell in love with weight lifting. As I pursued my love for lifting, it became very clear that most women didn’t know the benefits of lifting, were intimidated by it and the gym setting as well. It became my mission to teach both men, women and kids how to properly and safely lift weights.

Obstacle Course Races (OCR’s) are a big part of my favorite outdoor activities. I train and accompany various teams of ALL skill levels (beginners included) to OCR's all over Texas and the U.S.! Train hard, play hard!
