What is Dynamo Fitness?

Dynamo Fitness is a private gym and obstacle course race training facility in Pflugerville, Texas. It is owned and operated by Keri Klumker. All workouts are specifically made for EACH client and held in a private and comfortable environment. Workouts are tailored to meet individual goals and preferences. Safety, proper form, achievable goals and learning to love fitness are the core of my training programs.
